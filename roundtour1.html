Day 1


Welcome to Sri Lanka, on arrival in the morning at Katunayake Bandaranayake International Airport you are welcome in paging area. Thereafter, proceed to go your respective hotel in Dambulla area. On the way to Dambulla visit,


  • Elephant Orphanage in Pinnawala or millennium elephant foundation at Pinnawala.

  • Dambulla Cave temple

  • Airport Taxi from Colombo airport to Arugambay


Day end overnight stay at hotel in Dambulla.

Day 2


After the breakfast, morning visit Sigiriya and then drive to Kandy.


  • Sigiriya (Lion Rock)

  • Village Tour

  • Spice Garden at Mathale

  • Evening visit performance of Cultural Show


Day end overnight stay at hotel in Kandy.

Day 3


After the breakfast, morning visit Kandy and then drive to Nuwara Eliya.


  • Dalada Maligawa – The Temple of the Sacred Tooth Relic

  • Visit Gem Museum, Wood carving Factory and Batik Factory

  • Peradeniya Botanical Garden

  • Tea Factory, Tea Estate

  • Water Falls


Day end overnight stay at hotel in Nuwara Eliya.

Day 4


After the breakfast, start to go Ella by train. Thereafter according to your choice, drive to Udawalawa or Yala.


  • Train journey to Ella

  • Nine Arch Bridge

  • Herbal massage in Ayurvedic Spa


Day end overnight stay at Udawalawa or Yala (Depend on your selection).

Day 5


At Udawalawa or Yala,


  • Early in the morning safari at Udawalawa or Yala National park

  • Drop to Southern coast (Tangalle, Mirissa, Unawatuna, Hikkaduwa, Bentota)


Day end overnight stay at hotel in Nuwara Eliya.

Tour End.


Package including transport, accommodation (one double room with breakfast) for 4 nights in 3star hotels for two persons
