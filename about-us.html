About Me!

I’m Vishva Tharaka who is the owner of VTSL and “Vishva Tours Sri Lanka” is my property. I am a chauffeur guide in Sri Lanka for seven years. I have an authorized tourist chauffeur license issued by National Tourists Drivers Association (NTDA). First of all let me intimate you about the beginning of “VTSL”. After a long journey work with number of tourist companies, the temper day by day which I obtain from all the past experiences within my career life, finally I built my own business.

I start my career in 2013 and I launch VTSL officially in 2017 with my strong working crew. I and my team members work in tourist & hospitality industry more than seven years. Our main objective is to provide smashing travel experience along with the remarkable memories for our beloved guests during their travel experience in this beautiful country of Sri Lanka because the success of VTSL truly depends on the satisfaction of our guest.

Therefore VTSL try to give best of the best for our guests always. Not only that, to accomplish your dream tour in Sri Lanka VTSL allocated our flexible drivers and guides for serve you in a professional manner. That’s why you choose VTSL as your tour partner. Throughout our journey, we have gained a lot of trust from our guests and lovely reviews which make us confident in our industry. As a VTSL team we are looking forward to serve you to make your vacation wonderful & remarkable.
