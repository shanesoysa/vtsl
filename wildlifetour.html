Day 1


Welcome to Sri Lanka, on arrival at Katunayake Bandaranayake International Airport you are welcome in paging area. Thereafter, proceed to your respective hotel. In Negombo visit,


  • Fish Market

  • St. Mary’s Church

  • Lagoon view and Dutch cannel

  • Spend time in leisure


Overnight stay at hotel in Negombo

Day 2


AAfter the breakfast, start to go Kithulgala


  • Water Rafting

  • Water Canyoning

  • Spend time in leisure


Day end overnight stay at hotel in Kithulgala

Day 3


After the breakfast, drive to Nuwara Eliya


  • Tea Estate

  • St. Claire water fall

  • Devon water fall in Thalawakale

  • Herbal massage in Ayurvedic Spa

  • Spend time in leisure


Day end overnight stay at hotel in Nuwara Eliya

Day 4


Early in the morning visit Horton plains National park and start to go Ella by train, later drive to Thissamaharamaya


  • Early in the morning visit Horton plains National park

  • Train journey to Ella ( one of world’s most scenic train journey)

  • Drive to Thissamaharamaya


Day end overnight stay at hotel in Thissamaharamaya

Day 5


Early in the morning visit wild safari at Yala National park and end of the safari back to hotel then later ready to go Udawalawa.


  • Early in the morning safari at Yala National park

  • Visit Elephant Transit Home

  • Evening spend time in leisure



Day end overnight stay at hotel in Udawalawa

Day 6


Early in the morning visit wild safari at Udawalawa National park and end of the safari back to hotel then ready to go Deniyaya.


  • Early in the morning safari at Udawalawa National park

  • Evening spend time in leisure


Day end overnight stay at hotel in Deniyaya

Day 7


After the breakfast, morning visit Singharaja Rainforrest


  • Complete a full day in the Singharaja Rainforrest (for tracking driver will arrange you a tracker or guide)

  • Spend time in leisure


Day end overnight stay at same hotel in Deniyaya

Day 8


After the breakfast, drive to go Airport


  • Drop to Airport
