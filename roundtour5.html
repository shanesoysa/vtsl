Day 1


Welcome to Sri Lanka, on arrival at Katunayake Bandaranayake International Airport you are welcome in paging area. Thereafter, proceed to your respective hotel in Negombo.In Negombo,


  • Fish Market

  • Agurukaramulla Bhuddist Temple

  • St. Mary’s Church

  • Spend time in leisure


Overnight stay at hotel in Negombo

Day 2


AAfter the breakfast, start to go Dambulla. On the way to Dambulla visit,


  • Elephant Orphanage in Pinnawala or millennium elephant foundation at Pinnawala.

  • Dambulla Cave temple

  • Village Tour to get tropical village experience ( Ride in bullock cart through paddy fields, cross the lake by canoeing boat to the farm and you can have traditional Sri Lankan foods “ Rotti” from typical Srilankan kitchen with cooking methods)


Day end overnight stay at hotel in Dambulla

Day 3


After the breakfast, morning visit Sigiriya and Polonnaruwa then back to Dambulla.


  • Sigiriya (Lion Rock)

  • Polonnaruwa Ancient city

  • Herbal massage in good Ayurvedic Spa


Day end overnight stay at hotel in Dambulla (Same hotel)

Day 4


After the breakfast, drive to Kandy.


  • Dambulla Golden Temple

  • Peradeniya Botanical Garden

  • Evening visit performance of Cultural Show


Day end overnight stay at hotel in Kandy

Day 5


After the breakfast,visit Kandy city and next start to go Siripade (Adams peak).


  • Dalada Maligawa – The Temple of the Sacred Tooth Relic

  • Visit Gem Museum, Wood carving Factory and Batik Factory

  • Tea Factory, Tea Estate

  • Rafting in Kithulgala


Day end overnight stay at hotel in Nallathanniya (start to climb siripade at 2.00 am)

Day 6


After the breakfast, drive to Nuwara Eliya.


  • Tea Estate

  • St. Claire water fall

  • Devon water fall in Thalawakale

  • Herbal massage in Ayurvedic Spa

  • Spend time in leisure


Day end overnight stay at hotel in Nuwara Eliya

Day 7


After the breakfast, start to go Ella by train.


  • Early in the morning visit Horton plains National park

  • Train journey to Ella ( one of world’s most scenic train journey )

  • Nine Arch Bridge


Day end overnight stay at hotel in Ella

Day 8


After the breakfast, drive to Yala.


  • Visit little Adams peak

  • Rawana Water fall

  • Buduruwagala Temple

  • Katharagama Temple


Day end overnight stay at hotel in Yala

Day 9


Early in the morning visit wild safari at Yala National park and end of the safari back to hotel then ready to go Mirissa. On the way to Mirissa visit,


  • Early in the morning safari at Yala National park

  • Blow Hole at Dickwella

  • Dewundara Light House

  • Evening spend time in leisure


Day end overnight stay at hotel in Mirissa

Day 10


Early in the morning in Mirissa,


  • Whales watching

  • Spend time in leisure

  • Beach Relax


Day end overnight stay at hotel in Mirissa

Day 11


After the breakfast, start to go Hikkaduwa. On the way to Hikkaduwa visit,


  • Jungle Beach

  • Galle Fort

  • If you like, possible to do diving and snorkeling at Hikkaduwa Beach

  • Boat Ride (Ride in a glass – bottomed boat to view coral reef)

  • Evening spend time in leisure


Day end overnight stay at Hikkaduwa hotel

Day 12


After the breakfast, as your prefer can leave from the hotel at noon to go Airport. Before go to airport visit,


  • Boat safari at Balapitaya Madu River.

  • Turtle Farm

  • Drop to Airport on your departure time.



Tour End.


Package including transport, accommodation (one double room with breakfast) for 8 nights in 3star hotels for two persons
